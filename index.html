How to play

Players:

- 3

Goal:

- Player with the most points when the timer's up wins.

Setup:

- Space is in utter chaos with killer asteroids coming from all directions making it nearly impossible to get through without getting hit by an aseteroid.

- Multiplayer game wherein each player controls a rocket.

Objective:

- Get to the top of the map by avoiding the space asteroids and meteoroid attacks to make maximum number of passes within the time limit.

Rules:

- Doing one pass without getting struck by obstacles enables the player to go back to the initial position in order to do more passes.

- Game ends as soon as the timer is completed.

- Winner of the game is the player who has most points (has the most passes).


Scoring System :

  • +5 points to reach the top
  • -1 point when hit by an astoroid
  • -5 point when hit by a meteoroid
