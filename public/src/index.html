How to play

CONTROLS:

- ARROW KEYS for movement of Rocket

- SPACE BAR for BOOST!


GOAL:

- Rack up points to be amongst the top 5 players on the leaderboard.

OBJECTIVE:

- Get to the top of the map by avoiding the space asteroids and meteoroid attacks to make maximum number of passes within the time limit.

RULES:

- Complete as many passes,by dodging obstacles before timer ends.


POINTS SYSTEM:

- For crossing and reaching the top.

+5 points

- For hitting debris.

-1 points

- For hitting fire meteors.

-5 points
